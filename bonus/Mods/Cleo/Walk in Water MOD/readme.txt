This mod allow you to walk in water.But, you cannot walk
 so far or you will be sink

 _HOW to USE_=

 When play the game type 'WATER' not use ''
 if you go out of the water you have to type 'NON' for deactive the mod.

 _HOW to INSTALL_=

 just put the 'walk in water.cs' file in to CLEO folder.
 this mod also can be use in top of building.
